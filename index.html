Trail Talk

Growing up in New Hampshire there was no shortage of beautiful mountains to explore. I have to admit that I didn't take advantage of the White Mountains or the North Country as much as I should have and it wasn't until I moved away from the mountains and into the flat Mid-West that I realized how important hiking is to me.
On a long solo hike through Crawford Notch I decided to combine my love of hiking with my love of programming. I started to plan a unique video series, a series for people who love the outdoors and spending time at the keyboard.
In each video I will be chatting with myself, or my hiking partner, about the great outdoors, general programming, some of my challenges and how I've overcome them.

This page is still underdevelopment, please come back soon for more videos.
