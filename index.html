Trail Talk

I've never been good at creating things with my hands. Need a birdhouse? But, programming has changed that for me. I love programming becasue it allows me to create something new.

Growing up in New Hampshire there was no shortage of beautiful mountains to explore. On solo hike in the White Mountains I decided to combine my love of hiking with my love of programming. I started to plan a video series, a series for people who love spending time outdoors and at the keyboard.

In each video I will be chatting with myself, or my hiking partner, about the great outdoors, general programming, challenges and how I've overcome them.

This page is still underdevelopment, please come back soon for more videos.
